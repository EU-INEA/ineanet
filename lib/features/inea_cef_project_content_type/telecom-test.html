The Connecting Europe Facility (CEF) supports trans-European networks and infrastructures which fill in the missing links of Europe´s energy, transport and telecommunications sectors. A budget of €870 million is earmarked for trans-European digital services for 2014-2020.

This page contains the projects supported under the Connecting Europe Facility in the Telecom sector, arranged by call area and year.

As more calls are published and more projects are selected, additional information will be added to this page.
